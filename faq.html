FAQ'S

How to Register ?


You can find all the details here

Is test series compulsory?


It is not compulsory but it is good for practice.

Will test series be provided ?


Yes, test series will be provided from the month of September. You can find the schedule here.

Should i take the test on the same day as mentioned in the schedule?


No. you can take a test any time before Jan 31st 2017.

Will practice question be provided?


Yes. For every subject there will be 500 practice questions along with solutions. But only soft copies will be provided in PDF format which can be downloaded. Distribution of the material is a legally punishable offense as they are all copyrighted.

Will aptitude, english and mathematics be provided?


Yes, aptitude, english and mathematics lectures will be provided and in fact aptitude lectures will be taught in such a way that they will be useful even for CAT, GRE and other examinations.

Will my doubts be answered?


Yes. You are supposed to ask the doubts from videos, practice questions and test series through emails. In every Email you are supposed to provide the link of the video or the question paper number in which you have a doubt. Other than this noting will be entertained.

Are GATE lectures useful for the other exams like UGC NET ?


Part of UGC NET syllabus actually matches with the GATE syllabus and so GATE lectures will be useful for UGC NET exam as well. Many aspirants of UGC NET have registered and got benefited from our GATE lectures.

Are the GATE lectures downloadable?


They are not downloadable. But you can watch them any time and any number of times. Sharing your access or trying to sell or distribute videos is a legally punishable offense. Earlier we caught some people doing this and they were punished legally and a huge penalty was imposed on them.

What if I face any technical difficulties?


In case you face any problems, you can email us directly and you will get a response with in minutes during working hours. As such, no one faced any technical problems till now.

How will I know if my current internet connection is good enough to view the videos without buffering?


You can login as guest and watch the computer network videos here to test whether your internet connection is good enough or not. We recommend internet connections of atleast 1mbps.

Is each and every topic taught by Ravindrababu Ravula?


Yes. Even though a team of experts from IISc and IITs work with him to produce the quality material, all the classes will be taught by Ravindrababu himself.

How much time do I need to spend for the GATE preparation?


It actually depends on various parameters like your educational background, your learning ability, and your current work environment. But on average if you can spend around 300 to 500 hours watching the videos and revising what you have learnt, you will stand a chance even if you are starting from scratch. All said and done, we really encourage you to spend as much time as possible without taking any chances. It is always better to give everything you got if you want to achieve it.

How many months should I take to complete the preparation? Should I spread the hours over many months or can I do it in a couple of months?


Again it varies from person to person. We have even seen people prepare for only one month and got a good score in GATE. But, we suggest that you spread your preparation over as many months as possible so that the concepts will have enough time to sink into your brain.

Is coaching very important to crack GATE and get into IITs?


It is just like a sport where a coach will help you cut short your learning time using his experience. Ravindra has helped many students achieve great success with his experience. Coaching will definitely help you save lot of time and avoid lots of mistakes. You might hear some people saying that you can crack GATE with out coaching but it is estimated that nearly 20000 students are taking GATE CS after taking coaching from some coaching centres. If you are preparing only for one or two years and if you are not taking coaching, it is like running a race while others are on motor bikes. People who are suggesting that you prepare on your own are not going to take GATE on your behalf. It is your life and we suggest that you do not take any chances.

Do I need to prepare from any textbooks or the videos enough?


Our videos will be self sufficient for the GATE exam, but in case if you get time, it is better to refer to textbook as well. It is not mandatory.

Will all the previous GATE questions be solved in your videos?


Yes, all the previous GATE questions will be solved along with lots of examples to make the concepts clear.

Do I have to watch all the videos regularly?


It is not required that you watch them daily. You can do it at your own convenience. Any time, any place, any computer.

What is the total duration of the videos ?


They will be roughly around 350 hours.

Can I access the videos in my college using the college wifi?


Some colleges block the videos using the firewalls and proxys. We suggest that you talk to your network admin of your college to know about it. Most of the colleges allow vimeo but some block it.
